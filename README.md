# CrossFit Virtual Coaching Business

This directory contains all the files and resources for our virtual CrossFit coaching business.

## Folder Structure

- **/01_Knowledge_Base**: Contains the foundational articles and research that our coaching methodology is built upon.
  - **/Methodology**: Core principles and foundational writings (e.g., Glassman's articles).
  - **/Science**: Peer-reviewed scientific papers and reviews.
  - **/Programming**: Articles and guides on programming theory and periodization.
  - **/Nutrition**: Nutrition guides and meal plans.
  - **/Business_And_Community**: Articles related to the business and social aspects of CrossFit.

- **/02_Business**: Contains all administrative and business-related documents.
  - **/Planning**: Business plans, meeting notes, and strategic documents.
  - **/Marketing**: Marketing assets, social media plans, and branding guidelines.
  - **/Legal**: Templates for client waivers, contracts, and other legal documents.

- **/03_Team**: Contains information related to the founding team.
  - **/Jim**: Documents and profiles related to Jim.
  - **/Teaganne**: Documents and profiles related to Teaganne.
  - **/Me**: A place for your personal documents and notes.

- **/04_Clients**: Contains individual folders for each client. A template is provided to ensure consistency.

- **/05_Programming**: The central hub for creating, storing, and managing client workout programs.
  - **/Cycles**: For organizing programming into multi-week cycles.
  - **/Templates**: For reusable workout templates and formats.
  - **/Daily_WODs**: For storing individual daily workouts.
