# Branding Guide: Unbound Fitness
**Company Name:** Unbound Fitness
**Tagline Ideas:** "Train Without Limits", "Break the Mold", "Performance Freed".

## Visual Identity (The "Nano Banana Pro" Vibe)
**Keywords:** Energetic, Modern, Digital, Limitless.

### Logo Concept 1: The "Broken Link" (Strength Focus)
*   **Symbol:** A stylized chain link that is snapped open or dissolving into digital pixels at the break point.
*   **Typography:** Bold, Sans-Serif, slightly italicized (to imply motion/speed).
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   **Primary:** Carbon Black (#1a1a1a).
    *   **Accent:** Electric Volt Green (#ccff00) or Cyber Blue (#00ffff).
*   **Why:** Represents breaking free from the "standard" gym mentality or physical limitations.

### Logo Concept 2: The "Unbound U" (Abstract/Modern)
*   **Symbol:** The letter "U" constructed from negative space or dynamic lines that look like a running track or a barbell path. It should look like it's lifting upward.
*   **Typography:** Minimalist, clean.
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   **Primary:** Matte Navy.
    *   **Accent:** Safety Orange.
*   **Why:** Looks great on an app icon or a hat. Very "Tech Startup meets Gym."

### Logo Concept 3: The "Ascent" (Performance Focus)
*   **Symbol:** A mountain peak that morphs into a heart rate graph (pulse).
*   **Typography:** Strong, block letters.
*   **Why:** Connects the "Engine" (Cardio) with the "Iron" (Strength).

## Prompt for AI Image Generators (e.g., Midjourney)
> "A minimalist, modern logo for a fitness brand called 'Unbound Fitness'. The symbol should represent breaking chains or limitless movement. High contrast, vector style. Carbon black background with electric green accents. Sleek, professional, suitable for an app icon."

## Usage Guidelines
*   **PDF Header:** The logo sits Top-Left.
*   **Instagram:** Use the "Symbol" only for the profile picture.
*   **T-Shirts:** Symbol on the front chest, Full Name on the back.
