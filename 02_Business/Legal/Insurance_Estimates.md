# Insurance Strategy: Remote Coaching
**Date:** 2026-01-20
**Status:** Estimates Only.

## Cost Estimates (2025/2026 Rates)
*   **Annual:** $180 - $300 total.
*   **Monthly:** ~$15 - $25.

## Recommended Coverage
1.  **General Liability:** $1M / $3M (Bodily Injury/Property Damage).
2.  **Professional Liability (E&O):** $1M (Covers "Bad Advice" claims).
3.  **Product Liability:** (If you sell supplements/gear - likely not needed yet).
4.  **Cyber Liability:** (Data breach protection - Recommended).

## Critical Riders
*   **"Online/Remote Coaching":** Must be explicitly stated.
*   **"Worldwide Coverage":** Essential if you have clients outside the US (e.g., Jim travels).

## Top Providers for Online Coaches
*   **Next Insurance:** Fast, digital, cheap.
*   **Insure Fitness Group:** Industry standard, includes online.
*   **Alternative Balance:** Good for "Health & Wellness" broad scope.
