# Jim Royce - CrossFit Athlete Profile

## 2024 Masters CrossFit Games (Legends) Performance
Jim Royce finished 29th overall in the Men’s 45–49 division at the 2024 Masters CrossFit Games (Legends), held in Birmingham, Alabama.

### 2024 Masters CrossFit Games Breakdown
The 2024 competition was the first year the Masters division held a standalone championship hosted by Legends. Jim qualified as one of the top 40 athletes in his age group globally to compete at the Games. His performance across the weekend secured his place among the top 30 masters athletes in the world for his division.

### 2024 Season Progression
Jim’s path to the Games in 2024 showed high consistency across all qualifying stages:

*   **The Open:** Ranked 158th worldwide (Men 45–49) and 1st at his affiliate, CrossFit Solace.
*   **Quarterfinals:** Finished 151st worldwide, maintaining his top-tier status.
*   **Semifinals:** Placed 38th worldwide, which earned him his ticket to the Legends Championship.

## Historical Perspective
While his 2024 finish was impressive, Jim’s 2022 season remains one of his highest statistical peaks, where he ranked 12th in the world during the Quarterfinals and 14th in the Semifinals. His move to CrossFit Union Square for the 2025 season marks a new chapter, where he is currently ranked 1st in his age group at that affiliate.

## Personal Context & Professional Life
In addition to his athletic pursuits, Jim is the founder of Last Mile NYC, a logistics consulting firm. He brings over 25 years of experience in distribution operations to his business, mirroring the same discipline and longevity he has displayed in over a decade of competitive CrossFit (competing since at least 2014). Jim Royce continues to be a high-level competitor in the Men’s 45–49 division for the 2025 CrossFit season.
